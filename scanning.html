Why US compliance matters now

California's CCPA/CPRA is actively enforced — and more states are following.

💰

Fines up to $7,500 per violation

The California AG and CPPA are actively pursuing enforcement. Each intentional violation can cost $7,500 — per consumer, per incident.

🔗

"Do Not Sell" link required

Websites must display a clear "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information" link. Missing it is the most common violation.

🛡️

GPC signal is legally binding

Under CPRA, websites must honor the Global Privacy Control browser signal as a valid opt-out. Most sites don't.

20 states and counting

California, Colorado, Connecticut, Virginia, Utah, Texas, Florida, Montana, and more. Compliance is a moving target.
